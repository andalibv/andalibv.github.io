Research
I am working with Dr. Seungchul Baek on high-dimensional statistics, specifically focusing on datasets where the number of features exceeds the number of observations. We have developed classification algorithms incorporating feature selection, tailored for high-dimensional datasets. Our work has centered on applying these algorithms to gene expression data in cancer research, where the number of genes (features) typically far exceeds the number of patient samples (observations).
